Indicators That Matter (and make sense)

Just because charities provide indicators, that doesn't mean that such indicators are actually telling of a project's performance.

10 May 2014

Indicators are imperative to measuring success, but if there is no tangible plan set up to measure these indicators regularly and accurately, charities can skimp on providing good and honest results as annual reports are due. Here are some important questions to ask yourself regarding indicators:

  1. Are the indicators easily measurable?
  2. How are they being measured?
  3. How often is the data collected?
